Any Icon Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Maksym Duchenko, a sole proprietor (Einzelunternehmer) established in Austria and operating under the name "STAYER" ("we", "us", "our"), the developer of the mobile application Any Icon (the "App"), handles information in connection with your use of the App. By installing or using the App, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Data Controller: Maksym Duchenko (sole proprietor / Einzelunternehmer), trading as "STAYER", Neustiftgasse 16/3/D8, 1070 Wien, Austria. Contact: support@stayerofficial.com.

1. Summary

  • Any Icon is a home-screen widget and icon customization tool that works primarily on your device.
  • There is no account. We do not ask for your name or contact details — except the email and message you yourself send us if you choose to contact support.
  • Analytics and crash reporting are OFF by default and run only if you explicitly opt in; you can withdraw consent at any time.
  • We use Google Play Integrity to verify that your copy is genuine and licensed (anti-piracy).
  • We do not sell your data and do not use it for advertising.

2. Information We Process

We practice data minimization. The following describes the maximum that may be processed depending on your choices.

2.1. Data stored locally on your device (not transmitted to us). Your widget configuration (the package names of the apps you choose for a widget, the selected icon shape, the "themed icon" preference, and generated icon image files), a securely stored license-status flag and last-check time, your consent choices, and a local diagnostic log (see 2.4). This data stays on your device and is removed when you delete the widget, clear the App's data, or uninstall the App.

2.2. Installed-applications information (processed on-device). To let you choose which application a widget displays or opens, the App reads the list of launchable applications installed on your device and their icons. This happens on your device only and is not transmitted to us.

2.3. License verification (Google Play Integrity). To confirm the App is a genuine, licensed copy, the App requests an integrity token from Google Play Integrity and sends it to our secure backend (a Google Firebase Cloud Function). The token is generated and decoded by Google and may contain signals about the integrity of the device, the app binary, and whether the associated Google account is licensed. Our backend uses the result only to return a "licensed / not licensed" decision and does not store the token or its contents. Legal basis: performance of our contract with you and our legitimate interest in preventing fraud and piracy.

2.4. Support communications. If you use "Get support" in the App, it opens your own email application to send a message to support@stayerofficial.com. When you send that email, we receive your email address, the message you write, and — only if you choose to attach the diagnostic report — technical information about your device and the App. The diagnostic report may contain: app version and build, package name, approximate app/data/cache sizes, device manufacturer, model, brand and board, supported ABIs, Android version, build number and security-patch level, system language, screen resolution, density and orientation, tablet status, battery status, the identifiers and on-screen sizes of your Any Icon widgets, and recent in-app events or crash details. It does not contain your name, the contents of other apps, files, or messages. We use this information solely to respond to and resolve your support request. Legal basis: your request and our legitimate interest in providing support.

2.5. Analytics and crash data (only if you opt in). Only if you enable it, the App uses Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics. When enabled, Google may process on our behalf: app-instance identifiers, device and operating-system information, app usage events, performance and crash data (including stack traces and device state at the time of a crash), and IP address (used by Google transiently and then discarded per its policies). This data is pseudonymous — it is not linked to your name or to an account, and we cannot use it to identify you. These features are disabled by default, advertising-related data (ad storage, ad personalization, ad user data) is always denied, and you can turn them on or off at any time in About → Privacy. Legal basis: your consent. We use no advertising networks and no third-party analytics other than the Google services described here.

3. How We Use Information

We use information only to: provide and operate the App's core features; verify that your copy is genuine and licensed; respond to and resolve your support requests; and, if you opt in, understand aggregate usage and improve stability and quality. We do not use your information for advertising or profiling, and we do not sell or rent it.

4. How Information Is Shared

We share information only with Google LLC / Google Ireland Ltd., acting as our service provider for Play Integrity, Firebase Cloud Functions, and (if you opt in) Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics — see Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase data practices — and with Google Play as the app store handling your purchase and licensing. Your support emails are delivered through your own email provider to our mailbox. We may also disclose information where required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property, or in connection with a merger or asset transfer subject to this Policy. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

5. International Data Transfers

Google may process data in the United States and other countries. Where data originating in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland is transferred internationally, such transfers are made under appropriate safeguards, including the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses and Google's data-processing terms.

6. Data Retention

  • On-device data is kept until you delete the widget, clear the App's data, or uninstall the App.
  • License verification: the integrity token is processed transiently and is not stored by our backend.
  • Support communications: we keep your support email, message, and any attached diagnostic report for as long as reasonably necessary to handle and resolve your request, plus a short period for follow-up and our records, after which we delete them.
  • Analytics/crash data (if enabled): retained by Google for the periods defined in its policies and our configuration (typically up to 14 months for analytics data and approximately 90 days for crash data), after which it is deleted or aggregated.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Any Icon is designed to be privacy-friendly: it has no user accounts, and outside of support requests we do not collect information that identifies you.

Depending on where you live (e.g., under the GDPR, UK GDPR, or CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or port your personal data, and to withdraw consent. How this works in practice for Any Icon:

  • Support data (your emails to us): because this is tied to your email address, we can locate it. You may ask us to access or delete your support correspondence by writing to support@stayerofficial.com, and we will do so subject to any legal record-keeping obligations.
  • Analytics and crash data (if you opted in): this data is pseudonymous and cannot be linked to you as an individual, so we are generally unable to locate, provide, or delete data about a specific person (consistent with Article 11 GDPR). You can, however, stop all collection at any time by turning Analytics and Crash reporting off in About → Privacy; after you do so and/or uninstall the App, Google deletes or aggregates the data according to its retention periods. You can also reset your device's advertising / app-set identifier in Android settings.
  • On-device data: you control it directly — clear the App's data or uninstall the App to remove your settings, license cache, and diagnostics.

If we cannot identify you from the data we hold, we may ask you for additional information; where identification remains impossible, we may be unable to fulfil a request. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data-protection authority — in Austria, the Austrian Data Protection Authority (Datenschutzbehörde, dsb.gv.at). We do not sell or share your personal information for advertising and do not engage in cross-context behavioral advertising.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, including encrypted local storage (Android Keystore-backed EncryptedSharedPreferences) for sensitive on-device data and secure (HTTPS/TLS) communication with Google services. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 14 (the digital-consent age in Austria) and is not intended for children under the age required in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

10. Third-Party Services and Links

The App relies on Google Play, Google Play Integrity, and Firebase, which are governed by Google's own terms and privacy policy. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites or services.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date reflects the latest version, posted at https://stayerofficial.com/privacy-anyicon.html. Your continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact Us
